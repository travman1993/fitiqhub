1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files placed on your device (computer, phone, or tablet) that contain information about your browsing behavior. They allow websites to remember your preferences and track your activity across sessions.

There are two main types of cookies:

  • Session cookies: These are temporary and deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These remain on your device until they expire or you delete them manually.

4. Your Cookie Choices

4.1 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit FitIQ Hub, you'll see a cookie consent banner. You can:

  • Accept All Cookies: Allow all essential, analytics, and advertising cookies
  • Reject Non-Essential: Allow only essential cookies; decline analytics and ads
  • Manage Preferences: Choose which types of cookies to allow

Your choice is saved for 12 months. You can change your preferences anytime by clicking "Cookie Settings" in the footer.

4.2 Browser Cookie Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data → Cookies

Note: Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

4.3 Third-Party Cookie Tracking

You can opt out of personalized advertising from third parties:

  • Google Ads: https://adssettings.google.com
  • Google Analytics: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
  • NAI Opt-Out: https://optout.networkadvertising.org
  • DAA Opt-Out: https://optout.aboutads.info

5. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature. Our Service does not respond to DNT signals. However, you can still control cookies through your browser settings or the cookie consent banner on our website.

6. Third-Party Cookies
