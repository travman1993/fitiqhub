Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data stored or tracked?

No. All calculator inputs are processed in your browser only. We don't store any personal health data. We only use Google Analytics to understand traffic patterns.

Are these calculators accurate?

Our calculators use scientifically-validated formulas (Mifflin-St Jeor, US Navy method). Results are estimates, not medical diagnoses. Always consult a healthcare provider for personalized advice.

Can I use these tools for medical diagnosis?

No. Our tools are educational only and not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult with a doctor or registered dietitian for health concerns.

Are new tools coming?

Yes! We're planning to add more calculators (water intake, protein, macros, etc.) and expand our article library. Follow our GitHub for updates.
