Weight Loss Tips
  • Track your food intake consistently using an app like MyFitnessPal or Cronometer
  • Weigh yourself daily (same time) and track weekly averages to avoid day-to-day fluctuations
  • Prioritize protein (0.8-1g per lb body weight) to preserve muscle during weight loss
  • Incorporate strength training 2-3x per week to maintain lean mass
  • If weight loss stalls for 2+ weeks, reduce calories by 100-200 or increase activity
  • Include diet breaks every 8-12 weeks at maintenance calories for metabolic recovery

⚠️ Important Warnings

  • Never eat below 1200 calories/day (women) or 1500 calories/day (men) without medical supervision
  • Aggressive deficits increase risk of muscle loss, metabolic slowdown, and nutritional deficiencies
  • Weight loss isn't linear - expect plateaus and fluctuations
  • Consult healthcare professionals before starting any weight loss program
