Daily Water Breakdown

Base Water Intake

~30-35 mL per kg (or 0.5 oz per lb) - foundation of hydration

Activity Bonus

+500mL per hour of exercise, +250mL for light activity

Climate Adjustment

Hot/humid climates add 10-30% more water needs due to perspiration

Sample Hydration Schedule

Morning (6-9 AM) ~500 mL water
Before Lunch (11-12 PM) ~500 mL water
