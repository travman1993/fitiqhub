Understanding Your Results
  • Different formulas exist because no single formula perfectly predicts ideal weight for everyone
  • Your frame size, muscle mass, and body composition matter more than any single number
  • Use these results as a general guide, not an absolute target
  • Consult with healthcare professionals for personalized weight goals

⚠️ Important Context

These formulas provide general guidelines but don't account for muscle mass, body composition, or individual health factors. Athletes with high muscle mass may exceed these weights while remaining healthy. Body fat percentage and overall fitness are often better indicators of health than weight alone.
